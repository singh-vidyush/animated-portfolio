# Design System — Neo Brutalism

## Design Style

Neo-Brutalism.

Design goals:

- bold
- playful
- structured
- memorable

---

## Color Palette

Background  
#FFFDF5

Text / Borders  
#000000

Primary Accent  
#FF6B6B

Secondary  
#FFD93D

Muted  
#C4B5FD

White  
#FFFFFF

---

## Typography

Font:

Space Grotesk

Font Weights:

900 → headings  
700 → body text

---

## Heading Scale

Hero

96px – 128px

Section titles

60px – 96px

Cards

36px – 48px

Body text

18px – 20px

---

## Borders

Default border

border-4 black

Large section borders

border-8 black

All elements must have visible borders.

---

## Shadows

Hard brutal shadows.

Small shadow

4px 4px 0px black

Medium shadow

8px 8px 0px black

Large shadow

12px 12px 0px black

---

## Motion

Buttons

Push effect

translateX 2px  
translateY 2px

Cards

Lift on hover

translateY -2

---

## Decorative Elements

Background typography includes:

FULL STACK DEVELOPER  
FREELANCER  
BACKEND ENGINEER  
GOOD BOY

Opacity should be low.

Patterns may include:

- halftone dots
- grid pattern
- noise texture

---

## Iconography

Icons from lucide-react.

Stroke width:

3px or 4px

Icons should feel bold and heavy.
